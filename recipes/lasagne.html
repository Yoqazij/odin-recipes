Vegetable Lasagne

Home

Description

This hearty vegetable lasagne is a comforting, colorful dish packed with layers of roasted vegetables, creamy béchamel sauce, and rich tomato ragu. Perfect for family dinners, meal prepping, or feeding a crowd, it’s a delicious way to enjoy a meat-free classic with all the traditional flavors you love.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 jar (24 oz) tomato pasta sauce
  • 9 lasagne noodles, cooked or no-boil
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add zucchini, bell peppers, and mushrooms. Cook for 5–7 minutes, then stir in spinach until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with the egg and a pinch of salt.
  5. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer 3 lasagne noodles over the sauce, then spread a third of the ricotta mixture, a third of the vegetable mixture, and a sprinkle of mozzarella. Repeat twice.
  7. Top with remaining noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15–20 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  9. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
